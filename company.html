Our Story

Doozie Client (Doozie Client) was established in 1998 as a joint venture between Toyota Industries Corporation and Indian partners. Over the past 25+ years, we have grown from a small manufacturing unit to one of India's leading engine manufacturers, serving customers across 50+ countries.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities in India combine Japanese precision with Indian innovation, delivering world-class engines and auto parts that meet the highest quality standards. We are committed to sustainable manufacturing practices and reducing our environmental footprint while maintaining excellence in every product we deliver.
